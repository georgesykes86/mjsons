About Us

MJ & Sons is a family run company specialising in bespoke carpentry solutions within the Greater London area. With over 25 years of experience working on residential carpentry projects, we pride ourselves on working collaboratively with our clients from initial concept through to completion — delivering high quality craftsmanship every time.

What We Do

These are some of our primary services but if you have other carpentry needs please get in touch as we offer other bespoke services as well.

Cabinets, Wardrobes & Shelves

Custom fitted storage solutions for any space

Windows & Door Repairs

Repairs to windows, frames, sills. Door repair, hanging or replacement

Kitchen Fitting

Kitchen fitting and customisation to match tricky room shapes

Wooden Floor Installation
